Our Mission

Protecting Nature Starts with Community

TrailKeepersYouth was founded on the belief that wildfire prevention and nature protection go hand in hand. We train families, restore habitats, and share science-backed resources across California.

From neighborhood evacuation drills to cutting-edge prevention research, every action we take is rooted in community trust and love for the land.

🔥

Wildfire Prevention

Training homeowners in defensible space, evacuation routes, and go-bag preparedness.

🌲

Nature Protection

Habitat restoration, fuel reduction crews, and partnerships with land trusts across California.

🔬

Preventive Research

Funding and publishing research on fire behavior, ecosystem vulnerability, and prevention strategies.
