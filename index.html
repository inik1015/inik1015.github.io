Our Mission

Protecting Communities Through Education & Research

TrailBlazersYouth was founded on the belief that wildfire prevention starts with informed, empowered communities. We educate families, conduct real world research, and advocate for safer policies across California.

"To educate communities about fire safety, promote wildfire preparedness, and support access to life-saving fire prevention equipment through education, outreach, fundraising, and community engagement."
🔥

Wildfire Prevention

Training homeowners in defensible space, evacuation routes, and go-bag preparedness.

🔬

Real World Research

Conducting and publishing research on fire behavior, community vulnerability, and prevention strategies.

🤝

Community Engagement

Fundraising, public education, and seminars with specialists in their field.

Who Am I

Meet the Founder

Daniel Chang

Founder & Student Researcher

Hi, I'm Daniel. I am a student researcher with a passion for understanding wildfires and developing solutions that can help communities become more resilient to them.

My interest in wildfire science is deeply personal. Having experienced major California wildfires firsthand — including the Tubbs and Kincade Fires in Northern California and witnessing the impacts of fires in Southern California such as the Palisades Fire — I have seen how these events can reshape communities and affect families for years after the flames are gone.
